Made in my spare time while on university breaks. The idea of this game was to create a simple fun multiplayer game for my friends that simulated playing paintball & laser tag. This game is nowhere near complete and has a lot of bugs, but since I've coded everything except the UFPS controller I'm relatively happy with what I've made so far. Also this was my first attempt at a networked game and was made before I did a networking course.

Controls

Standard FPS. W A S D move, Shift sprint, Space jump, LMB shoot, RMB zoom, Tab scoreboard.

Gameplay

Simple multiplayer FPS shooter with multiple game modes:

  • FFA - Free For All
  • TDM - Team Death Match
  • CTF - Capture The Flag
  • LTS - Last Team Standing (similar to counter strike)

I recommend changing the level in the 'Game Settings' to 'level2' as its a better test map with the vibe I was aiming for.
